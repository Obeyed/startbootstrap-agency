About

Here at munk.ai we specialize in designing advanced machine learning models that aid companies in making qualified decisions.

Machine learning, deep learning, and big data make it possible to solve tasks that are not solvable with conventional algorithms; image and speech recognition, and summarization of text documents are just a few of such tasks.

Three ingredients are required to design and train good machine learning models: data, computing power, and experience.

We can work with almost any type of data; text, images, or even sound. You name it.
